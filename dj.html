Starshine

Starshine has been DJing since 2019. She spins an eclectic upbeat selection of happy hardcore, liquid drum and bass, house, and more - anything to get her audience moving and grinning from ear to ear!

Starshine has played music live in San Francisco, Sacramento, and Los Angeles, and streamed on Twitch in 2020-21. She’s played at clubs, house parties, the beach - and can spin anywhere there’s a Pioneer DJ Controller or CD-J’s and speakers provided!

Streaming may be coming back soon; estimated late 2024-early 2025.

Starshine is available for bookings in the Los Angeles Area & beyond.
